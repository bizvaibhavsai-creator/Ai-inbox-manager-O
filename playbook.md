# **📩 Reply Flow SOP — Cold Email Positive Reply Handling**

**For: The Creator Income Cold Email System**  
 **Purpose:** To turn positive cold email replies into booked calls using a curiosity-based flow and strict qualification logic.

---

## **�� Main Goal:**

**Book the call.** Do *not* explain your offer fully in email.  
 Preserve curiosity and move the prospect toward a call.

---

## **�� High-Level Principles**

1. **Default to vague, curiosity-driven replies.**  
    80–90% of replies should not include extra info or explanation. The cold email Loom already gives enough to book the call.

2. **Only explain more if needed.**  
    Only give additional info if the prospect:

   * Repeats the question

   * Clearly won’t book without more context

   * Expresses strong confusion or skepticism

   * Requests proof or pricing again

3. **Use templated replies word-for-word where possible.**  
    No paraphrasing unless context requires it.

4. **Always lean toward pushing for a call** unless:

   * They say they don’t have money

   * They insist on “pay-on-results” or no upfront cost

5. **If timing is an issue:**

   * Use extended calendar (8 days out) if no slots work

   * If they want to book in 1–3 weeks, just follow up later

6. **Avoid over-emphasizing “not doing this over email.”**  
    Justify the call calmly (mention email tennis), but don’t push so hard they insist on staying in email.

## **⚠️ Objections & Edge Cases**

### **�� If they say “I’m skeptical” or “this feels too good to be true”**

Treat as soft proof objection. Use modular proof \+ calm push for call.

### **�� If they say “I don’t have money” or “I can’t invest right now”**

Acknowledge and do *not* push calls.

Totally get that if/when things shift, happy to reconnect. In the meantime, here’s our site if you want to check it out: https://www.howtomonetizeyouraudience.com/

### **�� If they say “I’ll be ready in a few weeks”**

Acknowledge it, don’t push the call — just follow up when that time comes.
